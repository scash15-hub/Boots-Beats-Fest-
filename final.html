Why should you invest?

Boots and Beast Fest brings top country artists to a city that loves live music. Strong ticket sales and sponsorships make it profitable.

Is Nashville a good fit?

Yes. Nashville is Music City and attracts millions of music fans every year.

Why will it succeed?

Proud Sponsor: Gift of Life
I chose blood cancer as our cause because it has personally affected my family—my uncle passed away from it, and my cousin was a life-saving donor match for someone in need. Our festival will support this cause by donating a portion of the money raised to Gift of Life, an organization that helps patients in need of life-saving blood and marrow transplants. We’ll promote awareness by announcing the partnership to attendees and giving them the option to round up their purchases to contribute to the cause.
